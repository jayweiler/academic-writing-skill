# Editorial Decision Log

Chronological record of editorial decisions made during the writing process. Each entry captures what was decided, what alternatives were considered, and the author's reasoning.

This log serves two purposes:
1. **Process transparency** — Documents the collaborative decision-making between author and AI
2. **Audit trail** — Provides evidence that editorial judgment remained with the author

---

## How to Use This Log

Each entry should include:
- **Date** and **section** being worked on
- **Decision** — What was decided
- **Alternatives considered** — What other options were on the table
- **Reasoning** — Why this choice was made
- **Who drove it** — Did the author choose, or did the AI suggest and the author approve?

Decisions worth logging:
- Choosing one framing over another
- Deciding to include or exclude a reference
- Overriding the AI's suggestion
- Narrowing or expanding an argument
- Setting editorial policy (e.g., "we'll use active voice throughout")
- Resolving a disagreement between author and AI
- Deliberately leaving a tension unresolved
