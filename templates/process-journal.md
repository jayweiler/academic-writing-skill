# Process Journal

Running record of how the AI-assisted writing process is going. This is primary source material for documenting the collaboration — capture what's working, what's not, and how the process evolves.

Unlike the decision log (which tracks specific editorial choices), this journal tracks the meta-level: the quality of the collaboration itself.

---

## What to Record

After each session (or significant milestone), note:

- **What was worked on** — Section, phase, specific tasks
- **How the collaboration went** — Was it genuinely interactive? Did the AI push back usefully? Were there moments where the process felt performative rather than substantive?
- **Process refinements** — Anything that should change for next time
- **Surprises** — Moments where the AI was unexpectedly helpful or unhelpful
- **Bias observations** — Did any of the documented risks (literature bias, confirmation bias, theme downweighting, etc.) manifest? How were they handled?
- **Context engineering notes** — Did context loss affect the session? Were state files effective?

Be candid. A journal that only records successes isn't useful.
